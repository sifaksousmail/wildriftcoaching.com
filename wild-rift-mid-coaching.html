What mid lane coaching fixes

Danny reviews your real mid lane games and focuses on the repeated decisions that decide ranked outcomes. The goal is to leave with a short set of habits you can practice immediately, not a pile of theory.

Wave control

Use the wave to create move timers instead of roaming late and losing gold for nothing.

Roam decisions

Know when a roam is worth it, when to hold mid, and how to track the enemy mid.

Objective movement

Clear mid, preserve health, and arrive early enough to help your team take space.

How the review works

Send your rank, champion pool, and one replay or book live coaching. Danny looks at the moments before fights and objectives, because that is where most role mistakes begin.

Wild Rift Mid Lane Coaching FAQ

Can Danny coach mid lane in Wild Rift?

Yes. Danny coaches mid lane players by reviewing real games and focusing on the decisions that repeat: laning, map movement, objective setup, teamfights, and the habits that cost ranked wins.

Is this only for high elo players?

No. Beginners usually need clearer fundamentals and fewer bad habits. Higher elo players usually need more detailed work on tempo, matchup knowledge, macro, and decision-making.

Should I book live coaching or a VOD review?

If you want direct feedback while playing, choose live coaching. If you want a calmer breakdown of one replay, start with a VOD review or a free VOD review question.
