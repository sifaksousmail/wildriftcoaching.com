Wild Rift Coach FAQ

What does a Wild Rift coach do?

A Wild Rift coach reviews your games, explains the decisions that cost wins, and gives you a clear plan for your next ranked sessions.

Can Danny coach every role?

Yes. Danny coaches jungle, mid, ADC, support, and Baron lane. The focus changes depending on your role and rank.

Can coaching guarantee Challenger?

No. Coaching can make your mistakes clearer and your practice more focused, but no honest coach can guarantee a rank.
