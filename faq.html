Common questions

These answers cover the basics before you book a Wild Rift coaching session, VOD review, jungle coaching, ADC coaching, support coaching, mid lane coaching, or Baron lane coaching.

I am having trouble with payment. What should I do?

If you are having trouble paying online, please contact me directly through the Contact page. I can help you find the best payment option or manually arrange the booking.

What is your current elo?

I am currently Sovereign in Wild Rift. I have also reached Challenger on the Chinese server and have previous League of Legends Challenger experience.

What role can you teach?

I can teach every role: jungle, mid, ADC, support, and top/baron lane. I have reached Challenger-level play across roles and have coached players in every role, so I can help with both role-specific details and overall macro.

Do you coach beginners or only high elo players?

I coach both. Beginners usually need clearer fundamentals and fewer bad habits, while higher elo players need more detailed work on tempo, decision-making, matchup knowledge, and macro.

How do I book a session?

You can book through the booking button on the website. If you are unsure which option is best, contact me first and I will help you choose.

Still unsure?

Send your rank, role, champion pool, and what you want to improve. Danny can help you choose between a VOD review, 1-on-1 coaching, or the 5-session bundle.
