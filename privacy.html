Privacy Policy

How WildRiftCoaching.com handles contact details, coaching information, payments, and support messages.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how WildRiftCoaching.com collects and uses information when you contact Danny, book Wild Rift coaching, request a VOD review, or ask questions about a session.

Information Collected

WildRiftCoaching.com may collect your name, email address, Discord username, payment confirmation, messages, current rank, role, champion pool, coaching goals, and gameplay or VOD links if you choose to provide them.

How Information Is Used

Your information is used to schedule coaching, deliver VOD reviews, provide customer support, confirm payments, understand what you want to improve, and communicate before or after a session.

Payments

Payments are processed securely by Stripe or PayPal. WildRiftCoaching.com does not store full card details, PayPal login details, or payment account credentials.

Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold. Information is only shared when necessary with payment processors, service providers, or if required for legal, payment, or security reasons.

Cookies / Analytics

The website does not currently require advanced tracking to book coaching. It may use basic cookies or analytics in the future to improve the website, understand page performance, and make the booking experience easier.

Data Retention

Information is kept only as long as reasonably necessary for coaching delivery, booking records, support, and legal or payment obligations. Gameplay links and coaching details may be removed when they are no longer needed.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Danny by email at coachmedanny@gmail.com.
