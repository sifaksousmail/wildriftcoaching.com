What ADC coaching fixes

Danny reviews your real ADC games and focuses on the repeated decisions that decide ranked outcomes. The goal is to leave with a short set of habits you can practice immediately, not a pile of theory.

Positioning

Learn where to stand before fights start, not only after the enemy dives you.

Damage windows

Understand when to hit front line, when to step forward, and when survival matters more.

Wave and recall timing

Stop missing item spikes or arriving late to objective fights because of bad wave states.

How the review works

Send your rank, champion pool, and one replay or book live coaching. Danny looks at the moments before fights and objectives, because that is where most role mistakes begin.

Wild Rift ADC Coaching FAQ

Can Danny coach ADC in Wild Rift?

Yes. Danny coaches ADC players by reviewing real games and focusing on the decisions that repeat: laning, map movement, objective setup, teamfights, and the habits that cost ranked wins.

Is this only for high elo players?

No. Beginners usually need clearer fundamentals and fewer bad habits. Higher elo players usually need more detailed work on tempo, matchup knowledge, macro, and decision-making.

Should I book live coaching or a VOD review?

If you want direct feedback while playing, choose live coaching. If you want a calmer breakdown of one replay, start with a VOD review or a free VOD review question.
