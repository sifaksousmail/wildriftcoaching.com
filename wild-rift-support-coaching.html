What support coaching fixes

Danny reviews your real support games and focuses on the repeated decisions that decide ranked outcomes. The goal is to leave with a short set of habits you can practice immediately, not a pile of theory.

Vision with purpose

Place wards that answer real questions: can we start, can we face-check, and where can they flank?

Roam timers

Move when your ADC and wave state allow it, not randomly because the map looks busy.

Engage and peel

Know when to start a fight, when to protect carries, and when patience wins more than forcing.

How the review works

Send your rank, champion pool, and one replay or book live coaching. Danny looks at the moments before fights and objectives, because that is where most role mistakes begin.

Wild Rift Support Coaching FAQ

Can Danny coach support in Wild Rift?

Yes. Danny coaches support players by reviewing real games and focusing on the decisions that repeat: laning, map movement, objective setup, teamfights, and the habits that cost ranked wins.

Is this only for high elo players?

No. Beginners usually need clearer fundamentals and fewer bad habits. Higher elo players usually need more detailed work on tempo, matchup knowledge, macro, and decision-making.

Should I book live coaching or a VOD review?

If you want direct feedback while playing, choose live coaching. If you want a calmer breakdown of one replay, start with a VOD review or a free VOD review question.
