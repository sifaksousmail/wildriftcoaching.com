FAQ

Wild Rift Coaching FAQ

Quick answers for players comparing Wild Rift coaching, VOD review, and 1v1 sessions with Danny.
How does Wild Rift coaching work?

You send Danny your rank, role, champion pool, and goals. Coaching can happen through live 1v1 sessions, VOD review, or a focused improvement plan built around the mistakes in your own games.

Is coaching suitable for beginners?

Yes. Beginner players can focus on fundamentals such as champion pool, farming, recalls, map awareness, and simple objective decisions. Higher elo players usually work on tempo, macro, matchup details, and cleaner teamfight choices.

Can I book a Wild Rift VOD review?

Yes. A Wild Rift VOD review is ideal if you want replay analysis before booking live coaching. Danny reviews your gameplay and shows the repeated mistakes stopping your climb.

What ranks do you coach?

Danny coaches players across ranks, from lower elo fundamentals to high elo Challenger Wild Rift coaching topics like objective setup, jungle tempo, side-lane pressure, and advanced decision-making.

How long is a coaching session?

Most 1v1 Wild Rift coaching sessions are booked hourly. VOD reviews depend on the replay length and how detailed the analysis needs to be.
