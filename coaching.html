Why Wild Rift coaching works

Most players do not lose because they need one secret trick. They lose because the same small mistakes repeat every game: late recalls, random fights, weak objective setup, unfocused champion pools, and unclear map movement. Wild Rift coaching turns those patterns into a simple improvement plan.

Danny reviews your gameplay as a Challenger-level coach and explains what to change first. The goal is not to overload you with theory. The goal is to give you two or three priorities that immediately improve your next ranked sessions.

Why Danny's teaching experience matters

Danny is not only a high-ranked Wild Rift coach. He has coached Wild Rift full-time for over 6 years, has some League of Legends coaching experience, and brings over 15 years of gaming experience into his sessions.

That teaching background matters because mobile MOBA coaching is not about copying a high-rank player. It is about understanding why a decision works, when to repeat it, and how to make the right choice under pressure. Danny's coaching focuses on decision-making, macro, mechanics, and long-term improvement so players can build habits that hold up across many ranked games.

Wild Rift Coaching FAQ

How does Wild Rift coaching work?

Wild Rift coaching starts with your real games, role, rank, champion pool, and goals. I look for the repeated decisions that cost you games, then give you a short, practical plan for your next ranked sessions.

Is coaching suitable for beginners?

Yes. Beginner coaching focuses on simple habits that quickly reduce mistakes: champion pool, farming, recalls, map awareness, basic objective setup, and choosing better fights.

What ranks do you coach?

I coach players from lower ranks to high elo. Lower ranks usually need clearer habits and fewer repeated mistakes. Higher ranks usually need more detail around tempo, macro, draft, matchups, and decision-making.

How long is a 1v1 Wild Rift coaching session?

Most 1v1 Wild Rift coaching is booked hourly. The 2-hour session is better if you want live coaching and VOD review in one deeper block. Bundles are better if you want repeated review, follow-up feedback, and a more structured improvement plan across several sessions.

What roles do you coach in Wild Rift?

I coach every role: jungle, mid, ADC, support, and top/baron lane. I have played and coached all roles, so I can help with role-specific fundamentals, matchup understanding, Wild Rift macro coaching, wave management, rotations, and teamfight positioning.

What role did you main?

I was previously mainly a mid/jungle player, but I have also played a lot of ADC, support, and top/baron lane. This helps me understand the game from every perspective and coach players in different roles more effectively.

Is the coaching only for high elo players?

No. I coach players from lower ranks to high elo. For lower ranks, we focus on simple habits that quickly reduce mistakes. For higher ranks, we focus more on details, tempo, macro, draft, and decision-making.

Do you only coach Wild Rift?

Right now, my main coaching focus is Wild Rift because I do not have much time to play the PC version actively. However, my background also includes League of Legends Challenger experience, which helps me explain macro, role fundamentals, and long-term improvement across MOBA games. If you need specific League of Legends coaching, contact me first so we can see if it fits your needs.

What is your current rank?

I am currently Sovereign on Wild Rift. I am playing on the Chinese server, where I reached Challenger with around a 70% win rate in about one month, then reached Sovereign with around a 60-65% win rate while playing solo queue only.

Can you coach jungle?

Yes. Jungle coaching can focus on pathing, early-game planning, gank timing, objective setup, vision, tempo, and how to carry games without forcing random fights.

Can you coach laners?

Yes. Lane coaching can focus on trading, wave control, matchup plans, recall timing, roaming, side-lane pressure, and how to convert lane advantages into wins. This includes mid lane coaching, ADC coaching, and Baron lane coaching.

Can you coach support players?

Yes. Support coaching can focus on vision, roaming timers, lane pressure, engage/disengage decisions, peel, objective setup, and how to impact the map even without high damage.
