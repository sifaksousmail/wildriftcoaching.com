Wild Rift Coaching by Danny

Danny is a French professional Wild Rift coach and one of the highest-ranked players in the world. Currently ranked Sovereign, he reached Top 20 on the EU Wild Rift ladder (his peak rank) and ranked in the top 0.01% on the Chinese server, widely considered the most competitive region globally.

Before dominating Wild Rift, Danny was also a Challenger player in League of Legends (Season 6), proving his elite-level game sense extends beyond mobile gaming.

With cross-game and cross-regional experience (Europe & China), Danny offers high-level Wild Rift coaching designed to help players rank up fast, improve mechanics, and master game strategy.

Most importantly, Danny is not only an elite player but also an experienced pedagogue. He has been coaching Wild Rift full-time for over 6 years, with some League of Legends coaching as well, and brings over 15 years of gaming experience. His coaching focuses on helping students understand decision-making, macro, mechanics, and long-term improvement rather than simply copying high-rank gameplay.

Competitive achievements across League of Legends and Wild Rift, including Challenger-level experience and Sovereign rank on the China server.

Why Danny's coaching is teaching-focused

Decision-making

Danny explains why a play works, when it fails, and how to recognize the same situation in future ranked games.

Macro and tempo

Students learn how to plan recalls, objectives, jungle tempo, side waves, and map movement instead of only reacting to fights.

Mechanics with purpose

Mechanical advice is connected to real game states: positioning, cooldowns, champion limits, and fight selection.
